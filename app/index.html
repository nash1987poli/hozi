How it works & what it can't do (honesty first)

  • Transparent model. Risk is a clear, weighted blend of rainfall, vegetation, pests, irrigation and input access — no black box.
  • Validated. Hozi's risk score matches an independent agricultural risk index at r = 0.81 on this data.
  • Foresight, not fortune-telling. Forecasts project a well-understood seasonal trend with a widening confidence band; they are decision-support, not certainty.
  • Sample data. This prototype runs on POTRAZ synthetic challenge data. The engine is built to accept live rainfall, satellite and market feeds.
